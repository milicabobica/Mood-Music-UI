What is Mood Music?

Mood Music is a music app user interface (UI), where AI suggest play playlists based on user emotion input.
It involves a clean UI/UX design principles with some indication of AI interaction.

How does it work?

Below, there is text area, where you can put in your emotion.
The input can be either a written text, emoji, an emotion, or a song.
